Our People

Company Profile

Since 1972, Uitenhage Super Steel (USS) has been at the forefront of structural steel manufacturing and erection in the Nelson Mandela Metro's automotive industry hub.

Our team of highly skilled engineers, fabricators, and project managers brings decades of combined experience to every project. We pride ourselves on our technical expertise, commitment to quality, and ability to deliver complex structural steel solutions on time and within budget.

From conceptual design through final installation, our people are the foundation of our success, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern manufacturing techniques.

Our Facilities

State-of-the-Art Manufacturing

Our manufacturing facility in Uitenhage is equipped with cutting-edge fabrication technology and comprehensive material handling capabilities.

  • Advanced CNC cutting and drilling equipment for precision fabrication
  • Multiple welding bays with full quality control systems
  • Blasting and painting facilities for complete surface preparation
  • On-site crane and heavy lifting equipment
